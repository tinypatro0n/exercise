How to progress ›
Start at 2 sets of everything; build toward 3 sets once the week feels easy (usually 3–4 weeks in). The one rule that actually builds strength: when you hit the top of the rep range with clean form on every set, make it harder — in this order: add 1–2 reps → add a little weight → move to the harder variation. Skip this and you plateau in about 6–8 weeks. Rest 45–60 seconds between sets.
Safety ›
Clear this with your doctor first if you have a heart condition, high blood pressure, a joint replacement, or haven't exercised in years. Soreness is fine; sharp or radiating pain is a stop signal. Drop to the easier variation any time — hands on a counter for push-ups, knees down for planks, lighter dumbbells for everything.
